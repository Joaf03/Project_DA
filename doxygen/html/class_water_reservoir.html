Detailed Description

The WaterReservoir class represents a water reservoir entity.

It stores information about a water reservoir, including its name, associated municipality, unique reservoir ID, code, and maximum delivery capacity.

Time Complexity:

  • Accessors (getters): O(1)
  • Mutators (setters): O(1)
  • Constructor (default and parameterized): O(1)
